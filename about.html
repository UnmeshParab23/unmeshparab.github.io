About Me

My name is Unmesh Parab, and I am currently pursuing my First Year Bachelor of Management Studies (BMS) degree at Aditya Institute Of Management Studies & Research. I consider myself a thoughtful, kind, and welcoming individual who values meaningful conversations and believes in listening deeply to others.

I have a calm and introspective nature and often prefer peaceful environments such as staying at home or spending quiet time by the beach. At the same time, I am adaptable and believe that my personality lies somewhere between introversion and extroversion, making me an ambivert.

I enjoy drawing sketches, gaming, and watching Formula 1, along with practicing racing on simulators. I am passionate about music and enjoy listening to it on high-quality speakers. I am also a car and bike enthusiast and take pride in making a good cup of coffee.

I believe in peace, personal growth, and the idea of a utopian world where people feel heard, valued, and included.
